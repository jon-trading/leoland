# Leoland Humane Consequence Model v1

Leoland teaches by consequence, not sermon.

## Core rule

Humane, fair, practical choices should improve life in ways the player can feel.

That means better:

- trust
- calm
- dignity
- belonging
- resilience
- social confidence

## Negative rule

Short-term selfishness, vanity, rigid agenda thinking, or neglect should not quietly win.

They may produce a temporary gain.
But over time they should create:

- higher strain
- alienation
- unfairness
- fragility
- a meaner national atmosphere

## Intended feeling

The player should learn:

- kindness is not weakness
- fairness is strategically strong
- social warmth is infrastructure too
- a society can become more lovable or more brittle depending on how it is governed
