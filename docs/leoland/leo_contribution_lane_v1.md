# Leo Contribution Lane v1

## Current contribution model

Leoland now has two clearly different layers:

- **canonical world seed** in `data/universe/`
- **Leo inbox ideas** in `data/contributions/leo/`

## What belongs in the inbox

- city ideas
- region personality ideas
- club culture ideas
- issue ideas
- event ideas
- values/theme ideas

## What belongs in canon

Only ideas that have been reviewed and chosen as part of the shared world backbone.

## Why this split matters

It means Leo can contribute often and safely without:

- destabilizing the engine
- overwriting established canon accidentally
- forcing every fun idea to become a hard mechanic
