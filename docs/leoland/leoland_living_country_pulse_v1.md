# Leoland Living Country Pulse v1

Leoland now has a visible country pulse layer above the world-state engine.

The point is not more abstract simulation depth for its own sake.
The point is to help the player feel what kind of week the country is having.

## What the pulse answers

The pulse layer helps answer:

- what emotional weather the country is living through
- where social strain is rising
- where hope and civic warmth are growing
- what is becoming more fragile
- what kind of society the player is helping to build

## Main pulse outputs

The current pulse model now exposes:

- emotional weather
- pulse summary
- national story
- best thing improving
- most fragile pressure
- institution spotlight
- cultural moment

These outputs are designed to be readable in a short mobile session.

## Design rule

The pulse should make the country feel more alive, not more complicated.

If the player feels:

- more responsible
- more connected to place
- more aware of who is under pressure
- more aware that humane choices improve social life

then the pulse is doing its job.
