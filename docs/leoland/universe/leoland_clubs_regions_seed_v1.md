# Leoland Clubs and Regions Seed v1

## Seed concept

Leoland clubs and football structures are valuable worldbuilding input.

Current seed interpretation:

- clubs act as cultural and regional institutions
- clubs can act as proxies for political and geographic identities
- sport may overlap with region, electorate, and national mood
- football structures may later help express local identity, rivalry, belonging, and soft ideology

## What this means now

This should be preserved as universe seed, not merged into hard gameplay or data-model rules yet.

## Likely future use

- regional flavor
- province identity
- civic pride and factional identity
- soft political identity indicators
- news/event flavor
- social mood or legitimacy signals
