# Leoland Country Seed v1

## Canon seed

Leoland is currently seeded as:

- a large North Atlantic island nation
- a member of the European Union
- population approximately `277,000,000`
- `49` provinces or states
- a parliamentary democracy
- a five-party political system
- a large economy
- a strong military
- a country with meaningful regional political variation
- a country shaped by major national issues and voting-demographic patterns
