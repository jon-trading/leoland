# Leoland Region Identity Model v1

Regions now carry more lived social texture.

## Each region now expresses

- mood
- trust
- wellbeing
- pressure
- belonging
- resilience
- civic pride
- youth outlook
- community warmth
- neglect feeling

## Why this matters

A region should feel like a place with a social life, not a box of stats.

The player should be able to understand:

- whether people feel proud or overlooked
- whether young people feel hopeful
- whether everyday life feels warm or brittle
- whether public strain is turning into neglect

## Design posture

These values should stay legible and humane.
They are not intended as hidden simulation sludge.

The goal is for each region to feel distinct in tone and social experience.
