# Leoland Institution Life v1

Leoland models institutions before party theatre.

This is deliberate.

## Why institutions come first

The game is about whether life works for people.
That is more honestly expressed through institutions than through scandal-driven politics.

## Institutions currently modeled

- schools
- healthcare
- housing
- transport
- local services
- emergency resilience
- community life
- civic information

## Institutional life signals

Institutions now carry:

- strength
- strain
- public trust
- fairness impact
- effectiveness
- morale
- heldness

## Meaning

Heldness is especially important.
It tries to answer a human question:

Do people feel processed by this institution, or held by it?

That is much closer to the spirit of Leoland than a colder competence-only model.
