# Leoland Tone And Voice v1

## Tone

Leoland should sound:

- kind
- clear
- emotionally literate
- slightly playful
- never sneering
- never preachy

## Voice rules

Use language that feels like guidance, stewardship, and reflection.

Prefer:

- steady
- hopeful
- practical
- humane

Avoid:

- cynical insider-politics tone
- dark satire as the default voice
- mean jokes about citizens or regions
- treating distress as entertainment

## What the player should feel

The player should feel like:

- this place matters
- people here are worth caring about
- my choices have consequences
- I am being asked to grow wiser, not harsher

## Narrative posture

When a decision succeeds, the tone should recognize who was helped.
When a decision fails, the tone should show what became more fragile.

The game should not gloat.
It should help the player understand.
